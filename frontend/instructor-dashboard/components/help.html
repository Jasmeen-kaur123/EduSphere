Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new course? ▼

Navigate to "My Courses" section, click "Create New Course", fill in the course details, and save. Your course will be ready for student enrollment.

How can I add students to my course? ▼

Go to "Student Enrollment" section, click "Add Student", enter their details, and assign them to your courses.

How do I track student progress? ▼

Visit the "Dashboard" to see progress charts, or check individual student details in the "Student Enrollment" section.

How do I schedule an exam? ▼

In the "Exams" section, click "Create Exam", set the date, time, and questions. Students will receive notifications automatically.
